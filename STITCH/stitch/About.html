The Story

Rooted in the
Red Earth

MAISON PEREEN began with a single vision in the late 19th century: to capture the untamed character of our limestone hills and transform it into an experience of unparalleled elegance.

Through four generations, the Pereen family has curated a legacy of patience. We do not rush the seasons, nor do we compromise on the meticulous hand-harvesting process that ensures only the finest grapes touch our presses.

Every bottle is a chapter of history, a vessel of memory, and a testament to our enduring relationship with the landscape.

The Cycle

Vineyard to Bottle

Budburst & Pruning

The journey begins with careful winter pruning, focusing the plant's energy on a limited number of high-quality clusters.

The Harvest

Our pickers begin at 4 AM to ensure grapes are harvested at cool temperatures, preserving their aromatic acidity.

Slow Maceration
